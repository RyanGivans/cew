03 / Drainage & Irrigation

Control water before it controls the property.

Drainage, grading, and irrigation work protect the lawn, planting beds, foundation areas, and hardscape materials. CEW evaluates water movement and coverage before recommending repairs or upgrades.

Drainage paths

Runoff, slopes, low areas, side yards, and erosion risks are reviewed to determine a practical solution.

Irrigation coverage

Existing systems can be assessed and adjusted so lawns, plants, and hardscape areas receive appropriate watering.

Finished appearance

Drainage work is completed with attention to rock areas, surrounding beds, grading, and long-term upkeep.

04 / Maintain

Keep the landscape looking finished.

Once installation is complete, CEW can continue maintaining the property with the same level of care. Maintenance is built around clean details, reliable service, and protecting the landscape investment.

Clean finish

Visits can include blow-off of lawns, beds, driveways, and hardscape surfaces so the property looks professionally finished.

Detail matters

CEW emphasizes clean edges, directional mowing, and avoiding clippings left in roadways or hardscape areas.

Ongoing recommendations

Maintenance visits can also identify landscape issues, plant needs, and property improvements before they become larger problems.

Process

From first contact to final walkthrough.

CEW keeps the project organized and understandable. Clients are kept informed before, during, and after the work, with communication based on their preferred level of involvement.

1. Meet

Initial contact, on-site meeting, and site assessment.

2. Plan

Design work when needed and material direction.

3. Estimate

Review scope, price, and project options.

4. Revise

Adjust design or estimate before approval.

5. Build

Contract, deposit, scheduling, and installation.

6. Walkthrough

Final approval, care notes, watering schedule, and aftercare recommendations.
