How projects move forward

From first contact to final walkthrough.

CEW keeps the process organized: initial contact, on-site meeting, site assessment, design if needed, estimate, review, revisions, contract and deposit, scheduling, installation, project completion, and final walkthrough.

1. Meet

Initial contact, site visit, and assessment.

2. Plan

Design work when needed and material direction.

3. Estimate

Review the scope, price, and options.

4. Revise

Adjust the design or estimate if needed.

5. Build

Schedule and complete the installation.

6. Walkthrough

Final approval, care notes, and aftercare recommendations.
