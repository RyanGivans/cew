About CEW

Design. Build. Maintain. Outdoor spaces built to last.

Established in 2020, CEW Landscape is a Northwest Arkansas design-build-maintain landscape company focused on project-specific, customer-driven landscape solutions and high-quality installations.

Company Approach

Thoughtful design, proper construction, and dependable long-term care.

CEW Landscape specializes in creating outdoor spaces that are thoughtfully designed, properly constructed, and built to last. Every project begins with understanding the client’s vision and translating it into a functional, beautiful, and sustainable landscape.

The team takes pride in quality materials installed with care, precision, and craftsmanship. No shortcuts are taken. Every detail matters, from layout and grading to the selection and placement of each plant.

A significant amount of thought goes into selecting the right plant materials for each specific project. Site conditions, sunlight, drainage, and long-term maintenance needs are evaluated to support long-term plant health and performance. Soil testing is used when appropriate to confirm pH levels and nutrient balance.

CEW Landscape also evaluates and adjusts existing irrigation systems to ensure proper coverage and efficient watering based on the needs of the lawn, plants, and hardscape materials.

Why It Matters

Built around communication and final approval.

Throughout every phase, communication is a top priority. Clients are kept informed and involved, with updates based on their preferred level of interaction.

Upon completion of each installation, CEW conducts a detailed walkthrough with the client to ensure full approval and understanding of the finished project. During this walkthrough, plant care requirements, watering schedules, and aftercare practices are reviewed.

About the Founder

Chad E. Walters, BLA

Founder & Owner, CEW Landscape

Chad E. Walters is the founder and owner of CEW Landscape, a full-service design, build, and maintenance landscape company serving Northwest Arkansas.

Originally from Hot Springs, Arkansas, Chad moved to Northwest Arkansas in 1995 to pursue a degree in Landscape Architecture at the University of Arkansas. After completing his studies and earning a Bachelor of Landscape Architecture in 2000, he began building a career that would span more than two decades in the landscape and engineering industries.

Throughout his career, Chad has worked in landscape design, project management, construction, operations, and business leadership. His professional journey eventually took him to Denver, where he spent three years coaching high school baseball while continuing to work as a landscape designer. Although he enjoyed his time in Colorado, Northwest Arkansas remained home.

After returning to Arkansas, Chad managed and operated a large landscape company for several years, overseeing crews, client relationships, business operations, and large-scale landscape projects. Those experiences provided the foundation for CEW Landscape.

Company Story

Founded in 2020 with a complete landscape solution in mind.

In 2020, amid the uncertainty and challenges created by the COVID-19 pandemic, Chad made the decision to establish his own company. Drawing on decades of industry experience and a passion for creating exceptional outdoor environments, he launched CEW Landscape with a commitment to quality, professionalism, and customer service.

As a design-build-maintain company, CEW Landscape offers a seamless approach by combining professional design expertise, quality construction, and dependable maintenance services under one roof. This integrated approach allows CEW to guide projects from the initial vision through installation and long-term care.

Serving both residential and commercial clients across Northwest Arkansas, CEW specializes in landscape design, installation, renovation, hardscapes, irrigation, and maintenance programs tailored to each property’s unique needs.

Today, the company continues to grow while staying true to its founding principles: exceptional service, thoughtful design, quality workmanship, and long-term relationships.

Our Mission

To create and maintain exceptional outdoor spaces that enhance the lives of our clients while delivering lasting value through design expertise, quality craftsmanship, and dependable service.

Our Vision

To be Northwest Arkansas’s premier landscape design, build, and maintenance company, known for excellence, innovation, and integrity in every project we undertake.
