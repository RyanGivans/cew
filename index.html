FAQ

Questions homeowners usually ask first.

A good quote starts with a clear understanding of the property, the problem, and the result you want.

Do you offer free quotes?

Yes. Send your project details, location, and photos if you have them. CEW can review the project and follow up with next steps.

Can CEW help if I do not know what I need?

Yes. Many projects start with a problem like standing water, dead grass, overgrown beds, or an unfinished outdoor area. CEW can help identify the right approach.

Do you handle drainage problems?

Yes. CEW handles French drains, grading corrections, runoff control, soggy areas, and landscape repair connected to drainage work.

Do you install irrigation and sod?

Yes. CEW provides irrigation design, installation, repair, maintenance, sod, and lawn improvements.

What areas do you serve?

CEW serves homeowners across Northwest Arkansas, including Fayetteville, Springdale, Rogers, Bentonville, and nearby communities.
